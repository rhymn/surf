Transforming Ocean Plastic into Marine Products

Welcome to unplas.tech, a project dedicated to creating marine-focused products from ocean plastic trash. We believe in turning environmental waste into valuable resources.

Our Mission

Every year, millions of tons of plastic waste end up in our oceans, harming marine life and ecosystems. unplas.tech aims to:

  • Collect plastic waste from oceans and coastal areas
  • Process and clean the recovered plastic
  • Create simple, durable products from recycled ocean plastic
  • Raise awareness about ocean pollution

Our Products

We create a range of marine-focused products from ocean plastic:

Marine Equipment

  • Surfboards: Eco-friendly surfboards made from recovered ocean plastic
  • Buoys: Durable navigation and mooring buoys
  • Floating Docks: Stable dock sections for marinas and waterfront properties
  • Kayak Paddles: Lightweight paddles for water sports

Marine Accessories

  • Anchor Markers: Visible markers for anchoring locations
  • Dock Bumpers: Protect boats and docks from damage
  • Marine Storage: Waterproof containers for marine use

The Impact

By choosing unplas.tech products, you are:

  • Helping remove plastic from our oceans
  • Supporting sustainable manufacturing practices
  • Reducing demand for new plastic production
  • Contributing to cleaner oceans and healthier marine ecosystems

How It Works

  1. Collection: We partner with coastal cleanup organizations to collect plastic waste
  2. Sorting: Plastic is sorted by type and color
  3. Cleaning: All materials are thoroughly cleaned and sanitized
  4. Processing: Plastic is melted and molded into new products
  5. Quality Check: Each product is inspected for durability and safety
  6. Distribution: Products are shipped to customers worldwide
