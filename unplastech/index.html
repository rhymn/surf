Unplastech

Creating Fantastic Products from Plastic Trash

Welcome to unplas.tech! We create new fantastic products from plastic trash. By creating new great products from plastic trash, we reduce the need for new plastics ending up in the ocean.

Our Mission

Every year, millions of tons of plastic waste end up in our oceans, harming marine life and ecosystems. unplas.tech transforms this challenge into opportunity by:

  • Collecting plastic trash from various sources
  • Sorting plastic by type for optimal processing
  • Creating fantastic outdoor products that reduce the need for new plastics
  • Preventing more plastic from ending up in our oceans

Our Products

We create fantastic outdoor products from plastic trash:

Water Sports & Recreation

  • Surfboards: High-performance surfboards crafted from recycled plastic
  • Buoys: Durable buoys for water safety and recreation
  • Floating Bridges: Innovative floating bridge systems for waterfront access

More Outdoor Stuff

  • Outdoor Furniture: Weather-resistant chairs and tables for your patio
  • Playground Equipment: Safe and durable play structures
  • Garden Planters: Large planters perfect for outdoor spaces

The Impact

By choosing unplas.tech products, you are:

  • Reducing the need for new plastics that end up in the ocean
  • Supporting innovative recycling of plastic trash
  • Enjoying high-quality outdoor products with environmental benefits
  • Contributing to cleaner oceans and healthier marine ecosystems

How It Works

  1. Collection: We collect plastic trash from various sources
  2. Sorting by Type: Plastic is carefully sorted by type to ensure quality
  3. Cleaning: All materials are thoroughly cleaned and prepared
  4. Manufacturing: We create fantastic outdoor products like surfboards, buoys, and floating bridges
  5. Quality Check: Each product is inspected for durability and performance
  6. Distribution: Products are delivered to customers who care about the environment
